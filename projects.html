Recursion

A narrative-driven immersive sim built in Unity

Overview

Recursion is a looping immersive sim focused on player agency, corporate surrealism, and philosophical themes. The game explores identity, control, and resistance through atmospheric worldbuilding and nonlinear storytelling.

Features

  • First-person immersive mechanics
  • Looping narrative with dynamic environmental changes
  • Custom sound design and original score
  • Built with Unity using C#, Shader Graph, and Timeline

Development Notes

Development began as part of a capstone university project. The game prototype demonstrates interactive systems such as access cards, branching terminals, and dynamic lighting.
